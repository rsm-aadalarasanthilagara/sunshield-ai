# Workflow Document
**Project:** SunShield AI
**Date:** April 18, 2026

---

## User Workflow (Customer Journey)

### Persona: Alex, Campus Sustainability Director
**Context:** Manages climate resilience budget across campus infrastructure and public spaces
**Pain Point:** Needs to decide where to install solar shade structures but lacks fast, data-driven tools

---

## Current Workflow (Before SunShield AI)

**Step 1:** Review heat maps manually
- **Time:** 2–3 days
- **Pain:** Hard to identify exact high-risk zones

**Step 2:** Evaluate potential locations
- **Time:** 3–5 days
- **Pain:** No clear way to combine heat, solar, and cost data

**Step 3:** Request feasibility studies / consultant input
- **Time:** 1–2 weeks
- **Pain:** Expensive ($5k–$10k) and slow

**Total Time:** ~2–3 weeks per decision
**Total Pain:** High cost, slow decision-making, uncertainty in prioritization

---

## New Workflow (With SunShield AI)

### Step 1: Set Budget & Load Data
**User action:** Opens app → adjusts budget slider → views candidate locations

**Time:** 10–20 seconds

**Agent perceives:**
- Preloaded heat map data
- Solar dataset (ZenPower)
- Candidate site list
- User-defined budget

**UI shows:** Interactive dashboard with map + controls

---

### Step 2: Automatic Analysis
**User action:** None

**Time:** 3–5 seconds

**Agent process:**
- Computes Dual-Benefit Score for each site
- Ranks locations based on impact
- Filters sites within budget

**UI shows:** Ranked list + highlighted top locations

---

### Step 3: Review Recommendations
**User action:** Reviews suggested sites

**Time:** 1–2 minutes

**UI displays:**
- Ranked site list (Score, Cost, Impact)
- Estimated energy generation
- People served
- Heat risk level

**User options:**
- Click a site → see detailed breakdown
- Click “Why this site?” → AI explanation
- Adjust budget → see updated shortlist

---

### Step 4: Generate Proposal
**User action:** Clicks “Generate Proposal”

**Time:** ~5 seconds

**Agent executes:**
- Uses Gemini to generate grant-style justification

**UI shows:**
- 500-word proposal including:
  - Public health impact
  - Energy benefits
  - ROI explanation

---

### Step 5: Decision & Export
**User action:** Selects final sites → exports results

**Time:** 10–20 seconds

**Agent outputs:**
- Final shortlist
- Summary metrics
- Proposal text

**UI shows:**
- Downloadable report (PDF or summary)

---

## Outcome (After SunShield AI)

- Decision time reduced from **weeks → minutes**
- Data-driven prioritization of infrastructure
- Clear justification for stakeholders
- Optimized use of limited budget

---

## Key Value Delivered

- Faster planning
- Better infrastructure decisions
- Combined heat + solar optimization
- Improved communication with stakeholders
